2021

Bioinformatics Research Intern

École Polytechnique, BIOC. Advisor: Thomas Gaillard. Proposed a robust method on protein clustering based on vectorization of the residue to residue connections which identified key substructures identifying different protein conformations. Used Pymol protein modeling, T-SNE, Hierarchical clustering, to perform unsupervised clustering of cylin-dependent kinase 2 (CDK2). Developed an almost fully automated technique which out performed instructor proposed RMSD and Dihedral Clustering methods in classifying the three major conformations of the CDK2 proteins with an F1 score of 0.93 to 0.54 and 0.79 respectively. Manuscript
